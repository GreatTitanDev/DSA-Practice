## 💡 Tips

1. **Don't rush** – Understand the pattern before moving to the next problem.
2. **Track patterns** – Keep notes on common problem structures.
3. **Review templates** – Use code templates from the `templates/` folder.
4. **Analyze complexity** – Always think about time and space complexity.
5. **Optimize iteratively** – First solve, then optimize.
